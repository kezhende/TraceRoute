About TraceRoute

TraceRoute

The motivation for this application was actually a nightmare. No seriously. Keith saw himself stuck in a cave tracing his steps over and over again, but ending up in the same place. So he envisioned TraceRoute an application that would allow for you to retrace your steps with higher accuracy.
